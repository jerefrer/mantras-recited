How often should I report my mantras ?

You can report your numbers as often as you'd like.

Each time you fill in the form you'll receive an email with a link to modify the amount you entered, so you could either:

  • use this link to update your previous entry every time
  • fill in a new entry with the numbers you accumulated since your previous entry
